SOC INVESTIGATIONS

In LetsDefend, my SOC investigations follow a clear workflow where I start in the Monitoring section to review new alerts, then move into Log Management to gather evidence from system, network, and authentication logs. I document my steps and findings in Case Management, and if an alert involves a specific host, I analyze its behavior through Endpoint Security. For email-related incidents, I use Email Security to inspect headers, links, and attachments. I then enrich my findings using Threat Intelligence by checking IPs, domains, and file hashes, and use the Sandbox to safely analyze any suspicious files or URLs. Throughout the process, I move alerts through the Main, Investigation, and Closed Alerts channels to ensure each case is fully analyzed, documented, and resolved properly.
